Our Story

✦

At Arabian Palace, we bring the true essence of Middle Eastern culinary heritage to your table. Our journey began with a simple passion: to recreate the authentic flavors of Mandi, slow-cooked to perfection in traditional underground pits.

Every dish we serve is a celebration of rich spices, tender meats, and fragrant rice — crafted with recipes passed down through generations. Step into our palace and experience hospitality that treats every guest like royalty.
