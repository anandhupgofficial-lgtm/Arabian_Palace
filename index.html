Our Story

✦

Arabian Palace started its journey in 2017 with a simple mission — to serve authentic Arabian cuisine and become the destination for the best Mandhi in Kerala.

Our first outlet was established in Perumbavoor, where our passion for quality, consistency, and customer satisfaction quickly earned the trust of food lovers. What began as a single restaurant has now grown into a successful restaurant brand with multiple outlets across Kerala.

The introduction of our signature Spicy Kuzhimandhi became a turning point in our journey. Prepared using premium ingredients, traditional cooking methods, and a special blend of Arabian spices, it helped Arabian Palace build a reputation as one of the best places for Chicken Mandhi in Kerala.

Today, Arabian Palace continues to serve thousands of happy customers who visit us for authentic Arabian flavours, delicious Shawarmas, Broasted Chicken, and the best Kuzhimandhi in Kerala. Our growth is driven by one promise: delivering exceptional taste, outstanding service, and a memorable dining experience in every visit.

About Us

Who We Are

Arabian Palace was founded in 2017 by two college friends who shared a vision of bringing authentic Arabian flavours to food lovers across Kerala.

Their journey began with the first Arabian Palace outlet in Edappally. Through dedication, innovation, and a commitment to quality, the brand quickly gained popularity, especially for its signature Spicy Kuzhimandhi, which became a favourite among customers.

Today, what started as a single restaurant has grown into a thriving hospitality group with 15+ outlets across Kerala operating under three successful brands.

With a growing presence across Kerala, Arabian Palace Group continues to serve thousands of customers every day while maintaining its commitment to quality, consistency, and customer satisfaction.
