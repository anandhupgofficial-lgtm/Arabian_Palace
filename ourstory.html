The Beginning

The early days of Arabian Palace were marked by challenges, determination, and hard work. Founded by Manoj Mani and Sharafudeen, the journey began in 2017 with a single outlet in Edappally and a shared vision of bringing authentic Arabian flavours to food lovers across Kerala.

Building a restaurant brand from the ground up was not easy. However, the founders remained committed to their vision, focusing on exceptional taste, consistent quality, and customer satisfaction.

15+ Outlets

Signature Spicy Chicken Mandhi

One of the biggest milestones in our journey was the creation of our Signature Spicy Chicken Mandhi. Prepared using our own specially crafted in-house spice blend and premium ingredients, this dish quickly became a customer favourite.

Its rich flavour, perfect balance of spices, and authentic preparation helped Arabian Palace earn a reputation as a destination for the Best Mandhi in Kerala.
